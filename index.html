I absolutely love the design of this website! The aesthetics really reflect the handcrafted nature of the products. Each product page provides detailed descriptions and high-quality images, which are essential for online shopping, especially for handmade items. The navigation is smooth, and I appreciate the categorization of different crafts. However, I did notice that the website takes a bit longer to load on mobile devices, which could be improved for a better user experience

Roshan Sah

The website has a charming, rustic feel that fits well with the theme of handcrafted goods. The use of earthy tones and natural textures in the design is spot on. However, I think the site could benefit from some optimization. The image sizes could be reduced to improve loading times without compromising on quality. Additionally, incorporating more interactive elements, like a 360-degree view of products or customer reviews, could enhance user engagement. Overall, it's a great start, but there's room for technical improvements

Sumit Maurya

As an avid online shopper, I find this website quite appealing. The layout is clean, and the products are easy to find. Fixing these minor issues would make the shopping experience even better
